A HEALTHY OUTSIDE STARTS FROM THE INSIDE

Proteins in the diet are known as macronutrients, and contribute energy (calories) to the body. Each gram of protein contains 4 calories. There are 20 amino acids used to build proteins. Proteins that do not have all 20 amino acids are called incomplete proteins.

FACTS : VITAMIN A

Vitamin A is a fat-soluble vitamin that is also a powerful antioxidant. Vitamin A plays a critical role in maintaining healthy vision, neurological function, healthy skin, and more.

FACTS : VITAMIN B

B vitamins are a class of water-soluble vitamins that play important roles in cell metabolism.Though these vitamins share similar names,they are chemically distinct that often coexist in the same foods.

FACTS : VITAMIN C

Vitamin C, also known as ascorbic acid and L-ascorbic acid, is a vitamin found in food and used as a dietary supplement. The disease scurvy is prevented and treated with vitamin C.

FACTS : VITAMIN D

Vitamin D is a group of fat-soluble secosteroids responsible for increasing intestinal absorption of calcium, magnesium, and phosphate, and multiple other biological effects.
